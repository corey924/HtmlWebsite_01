KKTIX

KKTIX is an online event registration platform that allows event organizers to sell tickets on. Mobilogics has long been in a partnership with KKTIX who uses iPDT5 to scan e-tickets or paper tickets they have sold as event goers pay a visit to event venues. The solution can not only check tickets for validity but also input information about inﬂow and outﬂow of visitors to cloud data base.
