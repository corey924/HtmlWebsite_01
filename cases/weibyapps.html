Weiby i-Store: A POS App receiving orders from mobile devices

Weiby's i-Store is an iPad POS solution used in restaurants that integrates with dedicated and customizable Client Ordering App. With our aScan and Weiby's customized app, order clerks can receive food orders from smartphones or tablets directly without processing manually. This solution streamlines food order processes and increases customers' experience.
