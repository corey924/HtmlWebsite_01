Tsann Kuen, one of the biggest 3C retailers in Taiwan, has been implementing the largest beacon technology rollout in Taiwan utilizing Mobilogics’ MLBeacon to kick off. The 3C retail giant invites its customers to install an app called Golden Legend before they visit brick-and-mortar shop. When customers walk into a store, they receive a pop-up transmitted from the nearest beacon through Golden Legend that invites shoppers to walk around the store and engage in activities pre-programmed by the owners to acquire rewards and a cup of free coffee. Tsann Kuen's rollout is breaking a fresh ground in local retail markets and drawing in more shoppers.
