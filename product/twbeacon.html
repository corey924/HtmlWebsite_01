What is Two-Way Beacon

We introduced an exclusively modified beacon technology - Two-way Beacon. Two-way Beacon offers advanced connectivity of devices that communicate to each other over Bluetooth without Internet in an indoor environment. Two-way Beacon transmits pre-programmed signals to a user’s mobile device, so users can immediately respond to Two-way Beacon by tapping devices on their palms.

Two-way Beacon Outlook

A way to communicate to everything and let everything communicate to each other.

As Internet of Things(IoT) has become a trend for the future smarter world, our Two-way Beacon was developed for not only retail stores to improve customers’ experience but smart home where objects are turned into Bluetooth connected devices.
