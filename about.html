About Us

Founded in 2009, Mobilogics is an expert in "Appcessory." We named our product line as "Appsessory = Apps + Accessory", which enables our partners to operate business with mobile solutions.

Mobilogics provides companies worldwide with mobile solutions that reduce manual processes and improve productivity as well as efﬁciency. Our MFi certiﬁed barcode scanner and Beacon series streamline operations in a wide variety of markets such as retail, logistics, point of sale. Mobilogics not only offers ODM hardware appcessories but develops applications for various mobile devices. We attempt to satisfy our partners with both software and hardware services.
