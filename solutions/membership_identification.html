Membership Identification

Knowing your loyal customers' needs and wants in real time!

There is a slew of retail store owners looking for an advanced retail business solution to provide the steady stream of customers with better service. Mobilogics’ exclusive Two-way Beacon helps retail stores to provide membership identiﬁcation service that shows warm hospitality and offer welcoming service to customers visiting stores.

With the use of iPad/iPhone and your own app, Mobilogics' Two-way Beacon assists salesclerks to offer patrons considerate customer service by integrating with data-collecting network. Salesclerks can be instantly and wirelessly aware of what patrons might purchase according to customer record shown on mobile device. This service helps retailers build good relationship with patrons and improve customer experience.
