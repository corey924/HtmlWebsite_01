Stock Replenishment

Making stock replenishment process more effective

Stock replenishment is a process that occurs regularly for retailers. However, it is highly likely to make mistakes if inventory clerks process stock replenishment by manually tracking speciﬁc demands for stock.

In order to reduce errors, Mobilogics has come up with a mobile solution that allows you to synchronize collected data by emailing and uploading to warehouse data system. With the use of Mobilogics' aScan and iPDT5 as well as two apps available on Apple Store and Google Play: VZRstock and ezRestock, you can make stock replenishment easier and improve accuracy as well as time-efﬁciency reducing errors for inventory management.
