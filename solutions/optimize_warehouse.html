To optimize receiving procedure in warehouse

Stock receiving in warehouse requires a set procedure to check shipments and do paperwork when shipments arrive to replenish stocked inventory. The processes are usually complicated and take plenty of time to be complete.

Mobilogics' MFi mobile barcode scanners help you immediately scan shipments into facility's logistics system. With your own warehouse management app, receiving clerks can quickly inspect shipments and then input all shipment information to data system, thus improving accuracy and time-efﬁciency.
